AI Evolution Timeline

Journey through the remarkable history of artificial intelligence development

1950s - The Beginning

Alan Turing proposes the Turing Test. First AI conference at Dartmouth.

1960s-70s - Early Development

ELIZA chatbot created. First expert systems developed.

1980s-90s - AI Winter & Revival

Machine learning emerges. Neural networks gain popularity.

2000s - Big Data Era

Deep learning breakthroughs. IBM Watson wins Jeopardy!

2010s - Deep Learning Revolution

AlphaGo defeats world champion. GPT models emerge.

2020s - AI Everywhere

ChatGPT, DALL-E, and generative AI transform daily life.
